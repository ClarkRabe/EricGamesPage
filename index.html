Project Information

This project is focused on helping High School students understand the importance of argumentative writing by creating a game
that is both familiar and fun for those in and out of the classroom.

Project Description

EricGames is based of the popular JackBox Games video game. Key features include:

  • A wide range of interactive games focused on teaching argumentative writing skills
  • Will add more features upon further development
